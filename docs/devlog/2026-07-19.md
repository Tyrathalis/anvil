  First-attempt veto equilibrium held all run (0.078–0.110 argmax reads).

  the heuristic-half games, which ARE τ=1 sampled play vs the eval opponent,
  read **0.5064 ± 0.0081 pooled** (3,837 games) vs ~0.52–0.53 argmax. The
  ~2pp sampling tax lands exactly at the signal: sampled play sits at
  PARITY with the heuristic, so the heur-half mean advantage is ~+0.006,
  not the ~+0.025 the argmax arms implied. **The mixed-opponent lever was
  mispriced by the τ gap — temperature is upstream of it, and the two
  compound.** (User's Pokemon-Pocket prior: same failure shape, larger
  magnitude at high entropy; ours is milder because the policy is peaked.)
- Push-through vs fix: τ-noise is a compute multiplier (outcome variance
  uncorrelated with credited decisions), and at desktop scale signal
  efficiency IS the strength budget — fix, don't push.
