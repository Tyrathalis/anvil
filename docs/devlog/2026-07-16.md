# 2026-07-16 — d6-run2 collapse: diagnosis and writeup

## What happened

d6-run2 (lr 3e-5 × 16 iterations, re-ask on, launched 2026-07-15 21:26)
**collapsed overnight** and was caught at the morning status check, mid
iteration 6 of 16. Stopped immediately (~9.5 h spent, iterations 4+ were
garbage). Ledger: entropy 0.18→4.40, per-iter KL(π‖μ) 0.009→4.40, ρ 0.99→
0.27, veto rate 23.7%→86.6%, iter-004 arms **0.085 ± 0.014**. The monitor
recorded every stage of the spiral; nothing acted on it — the §4 anchor was
a "named contingency," and the contingency fired with no one home.

## Diagnosis (user chose "hold — diagnose deeper" over immediate relaunch)

Full writeup in [ADR-0017](../decisions/ADR-0017-run2-entropy-collapse.md).
Short form:

- **Root cause: entropy-bonus runaway under near-zero advantages.** Mirror
  self-play + calibrated critic ⇒ PG advantages average ≈0 (the pg loss sat
  at ±0.002 all run), so the always-on entropy bonus (3e-3) is the only
  persistent policy gradient, and lr sets its compounding rate. 1e-5 climbs
  +0.003 entropy/iter (run-1 — same force, ⅓ speed, previously misread as
  "mild healthy exploration"); 3e-5 compounds to runaway by iter 4.
- **Positive feedback:** entropy↑ → KL↑ → ρ clipped → advantages shrink →
  less opposition → faster climb. Within-iteration traces show it clearly.
- **Attribution:** priority *choice* head led (0.081→1.14); the
  pre-registered X-head suspect was flat (exonerated); value head flat
  v-loss 0.32–0.43 (v0 drift is downstream of clipped-ρ targets).
- **Re-ask = amplifier, not cause:** spray → veto chains → iter 5 had 1.06M
  priority decisions (4×) for 480 games, 66% veto events, 103K chains ≥4
  (cap 8 held). Wall time tripled. Turns flat through iter 4 — early
  explosion was pure chains.

## Decisions (ADR-0017, design doc §4/§6 amended)

1. Guards promote to v0: driver halts/rejects ckpt on per-iter KL > 0.05,
   entropy > 2× iter-0, or veto rate > 1.5× iter-0.
2. §6 anomaly rule becomes two-sided (critic ≫ reward flags too).
3. Always-on entropy bonus retired → hinge floor (bonus only below ~iter-0
   entropy; exploration is τ=1 sampling's job).
4. run-2 chain discarded; `data/training/d6-run2/` kept as the
   negative-result record. Run-3 init = d6-run1 final ckpt (the 0.5083
   corrected re-ask baseline).
