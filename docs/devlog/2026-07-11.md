## Broke / surprised me

- **The plan's "~69% recoverable / ~31% ambiguous" estimate was an artifact
  of matching at the wrong level.** Matching option strings against each
  other (M1's measurement) sees duplicates as ambiguity; matching the
  *chosen plan* against *collapsed candidates* sees them as one action. Four
  orders of magnitude less masked mass than priced. Lesson: measure the
  label algorithm you'll actually run, not a proxy of it.
- The 4K-game sample read a 6.5K vocab; the full corpus reads 33.7K — the SA
  string tail is long (mode-bound modal variants, X renders). OOV stayed
  ≤0.05% either way, so the vocab-embedding decision survives, but sample
  vocab sizes are not corpus vocab sizes.
- `ln -s repo/data worktree/data` lands INSIDE the checked-out `data/pool`
  (force-tracked config makes `data/` exist in worktrees) — creating
  `data/data`. Symlink the subdirs.
- prefix-min join alone (without exact-first) converts 1.9% of casts into
  *distinct-string* multi-matches — order the stages: exact, then prefix.
