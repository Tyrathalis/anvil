## Broke / surprised me

- **AIVAT with an honest-but-weak critic makes eval WORSE at β=1** (var
  ratio 1.074): each correction is unbiased but its noise exceeds its
  signal at ρ=0.08. The classic control-variate shrinkage (β̂ = cov/var)
  is not an optional nicety — it's the deployment form; it subsumes pure
  AIVAT (β̂→1 as the critic sharpens).
- The engine's mulligan flow hides a decision inside what the callback
  stream renders as "the dealt hand" — chance-node extraction has to model
  engine mechanics, not rules-as-written. The certification apparatus
  itself is what caught it, which is §7 working as designed.
- Draw-class game-sum t=1.95 (node-level clean; ~7 t-stats computed →
  look-elsewhere-unremarkable). Watch item for the next pass.
